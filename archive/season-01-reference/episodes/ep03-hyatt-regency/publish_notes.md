## Description

A drawing change in the Kansas City Hyatt Regency walkway system looked routine. In the load path, it doubled the force on a connection that was already too weak.

This video follows how a shop drawing revision, hanger rods, box beams, and an ambiguous review process turned a fabrication convenience into one of the deadliest structural failures in American history.
