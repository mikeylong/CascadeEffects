## Writer-Packet Claims Supported

- On March 27, 1977, KLM 4805 and Pan Am 1736 were diverted to Los Rodeos after the Las Palmas bombing.
- Congestion forced runway backtracking; low cloud/fog reduced visibility.
- KLM began takeoff while Pan Am was still on the runway.
- Official findings identify KLM's takeoff without clearance as fundamental, with multiple contributing operational and communications factors.
