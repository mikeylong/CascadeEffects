## Writer-Packet Claims Supported

- On May 6, 1937, Hindenburg burned while approaching the Lakehurst mooring mast; 35 of 97 aboard died, plus one ground crew member.
- The strongest mechanism is not simply "hydrogen explodes"; it is prestige technology operating inside a fragile weather/landing/material envelope.
- U.S. and German investigations center a hydrogen-air mixture near rear gas cells 4/5, with ignition likely from electrical discharge during landing/grounding conditions.
