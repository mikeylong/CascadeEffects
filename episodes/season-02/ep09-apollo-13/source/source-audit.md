## Writer-Packet Claims Supported

- Oxygen tank 2 had a relevant preflight history before Apollo 13.
- The oxygen shelf had been removed from Apollo 10 hardware and dropped about two inches.
- Tank 2 showed detanking trouble during the countdown demonstration test.
- Ground heater power and thermostatic switch ratings created a latent damage path.
- In-flight fan stirring likely triggered a short/spark/combustion event inside the tank.
- Fuel-cell oxygen loss turned the lunar module into the practical life-support lifeboat.
