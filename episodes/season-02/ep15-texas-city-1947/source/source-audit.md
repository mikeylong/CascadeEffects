## Writer-Packet Claims Supported

- The disaster began with fire aboard the French ship SS Grandcamp at Texas City on April 16, 1947.
- The central hazard was ammonium nitrate fertilizer cargo under heat/confinement conditions, followed by catastrophic explosion.
- SS High Flyer later became part of the cascading disaster.
- The useful frame is cargo risk, emergency assumptions, dockside proximity, and communication gaps.

## Cautions / Open Questions

- Exact death toll varies by source; use "nearly 600" until final fact-check chooses one figure.
- Do not assert ignition cause as settled without tying it to the USCG/Bureau of Mines record.
- Avoid simplifying ammonium nitrate as merely flammable; the mechanism is decomposition/explosion risk under specific conditions.
