# Challenger Visual Act Breakdown

This package is an option board first: 6 to 8 opening candidates, at least 2 subject candidates, and at least 8 candidates per act before narrowing.

## Opening sequence contract

- Opening mode: brief generic 1986 culture-object intro that leads into act 1.
- Time period label: January 1986
- Subject object: Space Shuttle Challenger
- Supporting objects: VHS cassette, cassette boombox radio, white leather high-top sneaker, aluminum soda can, cube puzzle, beige home computer CRT
- Focus transition: The period objects drift away as Challenger takes the center of frame.
- Subject action: Challenger becomes the focal point and commits to liftoff.
- Planned scene / motion: `launch_commit_console` -> `launch_commit_dolly`

## Coverage guardrails

- Keep the contact sheet broad and reviewable; it should not collapse into a final hero selection.
- Keep the generic culture-object intro separate from act coverage; act 1 still needs its full mission-control option board immediately after the intro.
- Favor recognizable period objects across media, music, fashion, packaged consumer goods, toys or games, and computing over a narrow cluster of recording or communications gear.
- Favor contextual evidence over text-led diagrams, readable paperwork, or fragment-only forensic crops.
- Packaging and motion inherit from approved stills; they do not define the research board.
