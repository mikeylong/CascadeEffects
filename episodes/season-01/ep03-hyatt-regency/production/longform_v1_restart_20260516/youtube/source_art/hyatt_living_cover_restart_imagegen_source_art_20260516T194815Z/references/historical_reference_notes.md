# Hyatt Source-Art Reference Notes

Reference basis for source-art review only. This is not a completed fact-check packet.

- NIST/NBS BSS 143 documents suspended walkways in the Hyatt atrium, with fourth-floor and second-floor walkways supported by hanger rods and box-beam connections.
- NIST's public disaster/failure page identifies insufficient load capacity of box beam-hanger rod connections as the probable cause.
- ASCE's public article summarizes the shop drawing change from a continuous rod concept to a double-rod arrangement and the resulting load increase at the upper connections.

Visual target: pre-collapse atrium structure, not the collapse event.
