## Canonical Visual Facts

- Three suspended walkways spanned the atrium at the second, third, and fourth floor levels.
- The second-floor and fourth-floor walkways were directly stacked.
- The fourth-floor walkway hung from atrium roof framing.
- The second-floor walkway hung from the fourth-floor walkway.

- The walkways had rectangular deck/box-beam massing and glass railing panels.
- The atrium floor was hosting a tea dance, so balloons, tables, dancing, crowd density, and anonymous people on walkways are story-relevant.
- The scene must be pre-collapse only.
