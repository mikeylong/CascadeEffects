## Asset Brief

- Archetype: Folded Cascade Model.
- Carrier: built-in image generation from written prompts only, with exact-size local runtime derivations.
- Title/text policy: preserve only the baked `Cascade of Effects` folded-paper title; no extra copy, labels, logos, or watermarks.
- Composition: preserve the title, broken suspension bridge, folded paper mountain/cascade stage, endangered paper figures, shuttle stack, launch tower, leaning ocean liner, and ink-blue field.
- Peril treatment: figures are alarmed, clinging, reaching, and partially swallowed or occluded by buckled dry vellum sheets rather than sitting upright or sliding feet-first.
- Launch infrastructure treatment: tower, pad, gantry, support trusses, bridge, shuttle infrastructure, and cream cliffs must read as neutral cream/off-white/warm-gray paper, not lavender or purple title-color contamination.
- Smooth-paper treatment: clean folded paper/vellum sheets with broad planes, soft scored seams, foam-core cut edges, controlled model lighting, and subtle paper fiber only.
- Surface rule: stone, rock, ice, crystalline facets, rough plaster, concrete, visible grain, speckle, grit, sandy surface, noisy shadows, texture overlays, and texture competing with title or scene readability are blockers.
- Signal rule: blue cascade forms must read as dry cyan vellum/paper sheets, not water, waterfall, river, stream, liquid spill, flowing channel, reflective pool, or recreational chute.
