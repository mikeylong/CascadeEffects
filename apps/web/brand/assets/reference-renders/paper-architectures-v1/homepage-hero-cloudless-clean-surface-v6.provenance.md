# Homepage Hero Cloudless Clean Surface V6 Provenance

Date: 2026-06-06

Scope: CascadeEffects.tv homepage hero regenerated smooth-paper raster/source-art revision with darker title lettering.

Correction: v5 corrected the stone-like surface read, but the title was too light. A deterministic recolor attempt introduced an unacceptable purple spill beside the title. v6 replaces that attempt with fresh image-generated artwork: the title is native darker purple folded-paper geometry, while adjacent cream paper planes, dry cyan vellum sheets, bridge, shuttle, ship, figures, and ink-blue field remain free of purple backplates, haze, or spill.

Supersession: v6 is no longer the active homepage hero because the seated figures and smooth cascade panels could read as a water-park or amusement ride. Use `homepage-hero-cloudless-clean-surface-v8.provenance.md` and its regenerated v8 source renders for the active web hero. v7 remains the peril/staging rollback reference, and v6 remains the darker-title smooth-paper rollback/comparison reference.

## Asset Brief

- Archetype: Folded Cascade Model.
- Carrier: built-in image generation using the v5 desktop/mobile composition as smooth-paper reference and v4 only as darker-purple title color reference.
- Title/text policy: preserve only the baked `Cascade of Effects` folded-paper title; no extra copy, labels, logos, or watermarks.
- Composition: preserve the title, broken suspension bridge, folded paper mountain/cascade stage, three paper figures, shuttle stack, launch tower, leaning ocean liner, and ink-blue field.
- Smooth-paper treatment: clean folded paper/vellum sheets with broad planes, soft scored seams, foam-core cut edges, controlled model lighting, and subtle paper fiber only.
- Title treatment: darker lavender-purple folded-paper title forms with cream beveled paper edges; purple must remain confined to the letter geometry.
- Surface rule: stone, rock, ice, crystalline facets, rough plaster, concrete, visible grain, speckle, grit, sandy surface, noisy shadows, texture overlays, and texture competing with title or scene readability are blockers.
- Signal rule: blue cascade forms must read as dry cyan vellum/paper sheets, not water, waterfall, river, stream, liquid spill, flowing channel, or reflective pool.
