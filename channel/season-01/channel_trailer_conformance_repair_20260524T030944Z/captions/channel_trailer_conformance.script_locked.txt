Welcome to Cascade of Effects. These are stories about failures people think they already understand: Challenger, Therac-25, Hyatt Regency, Semmelweis, Tacoma Narrows, Piltdown Man, 737 MAX, and Titanic. Each episode follows the same question: what changed, who missed it, and how did the system turn that blindness into consequence? The pattern is never a single bad decision. It is a chain of assumptions that stays respectable after reality has moved. The headline is only the doorway. The mechanism is the story.
