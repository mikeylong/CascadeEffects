# Cascade Effects Season 1 Playlist Thumbnail Left-Title Paper Options

Status: `review_required`

This package creates eight additional 16:9 Season 1 playlist thumbnail options with a large deterministic title on the left and the kept title-free Paper Architecture evidence-desk render on the right.

No YouTube Studio/API action was performed. No candidate is marked `keep`.

## Candidate Directions

- `F_left_title_split_ink`: Direct split composition: giant Season 1 title on the left, kept evidence-desk Paper Architecture render on the right.
- `G_left_title_cream_card`: Course-cover variant with a cream paper title block and right-side render framed as a clean Paper Architecture plate.
- `H_left_title_diagonal_cut`: Angled paper cut between the oversized title and the right-side render to make the split feel more architectural.
- `I_left_title_banner_world`: Title-led playlist card with a broader right-side view of the kept banner world and lower-right overlay-safe quieting.
- `J_left_title_course_stripe`: Educational cover treatment with strong title hierarchy and a right render cropped high to protect the playlist overlay zone.
- `K_left_title_prestige_minimal`: Sparse documentary card: very large left title, restrained series text, and a cinematic right-side Paper Architecture anchor.
- `L_left_title_paper_stack`: Layered paper-stack title treatment with the render sitting as a large physical evidence plate on the right.
- `M_left_title_high_contrast_safe`: Highest small-size contrast: oversized Season 1 type, compressed subtitle, and right-side render with the overlay zone deliberately low-detail.
